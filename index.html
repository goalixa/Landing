FAQ

Answers to common questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

Is Goalixa free to use?

Yes. Goalixa is completely free with full access to all features including goal planning, time tracking, habit building, and progress analytics.

Does it work on mobile?

Absolutely. Goalixa is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on desktop, tablet, and mobile with offline support.

What's the time tracking feature?

Built-in Pomodoro-style timer (25-minute sessions) that tracks time spent on tasks, helping you measure real progress vs. planned work.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your goals, tasks, time entries, and progress data to CSV for backup or analysis.
